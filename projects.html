Genpact  ·  2020 to 2021
COVID-19 Transition Programme: Enterprise change communications
Change communications
+

"The brief was simple: keep 2,000 people informed, reassured, and connected through the most uncertain period any of them had experienced at work."

The challenge

Genpact needed to execute a 12-month transition programme while maintaining employee awareness, operational continuity, and psychological safety across a fully remote workforce, with no prior playbook to work from.

My role

Core communications team member responsible for channel strategy, message architecture, content production, and cadence planning across email, intranet, Teams, town halls, and manager cascade kits.

Campaign lead time, extended from 2 days, creating space for stakeholder alignment, message refinement, and channel coordination before every send.
What worked

Treating the communications calendar as a strategic asset rather than an administrative one. Moving to a 10-day lead time created discipline that improved quality across every channel simultaneously.

What I learned

Employees are not anxious about the news. They are anxious about the silence between the news. Predictable cadence is as important as message content.

Genpact  ·  2018 to 2023
SharePoint Intranet: Architecture, governance, and engagement
Digital channels
+

"An intranet is only as good as the trust employees place in it. Building that trust means showing up consistently, with the right content, in the right place, on time."

The challenge

Genpact's intranet was a publishing destination, not a destination employees chose to visit. Pages were inconsistently updated, ownership was unclear, and navigation did not reflect how employees actually looked for information.

What I built

A content governance model with clear zone ownership, publishing cadence, and archiving rules. Restructured the homepage architecture around audience need rather than department preference. Monthly active users increased by approximately 35%.
